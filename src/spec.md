# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a premium e-commerce website for Cosmata skincare brand with a complete product catalog, shopping cart functionality, and clinical minimal aesthetic.

**Planned changes:**
- Create sticky header with Cosmata logo, navigation menu (Home, Shop, About, Blog, Contact), and icons for search, account, and cart
- Build hero section with headline "Glow Naturally. Clinically Proven.", subtext, CTAs, and premium product imagery
- Add trust badge section with dermatological testing, safe ingredients, and customer support icons
- Create Shop by Category section with 4 category cards (Serum, Cleanser, Moisturizer, Face Wash)
- Build best sellers product grid (4 per row) with product cards showing image, name, benefit, price, and "Add to Cart" button
- Add ingredient highlight section showcasing Vitamin C, Kojic Acid, and Niacinamide benefits with skin texture imagery
- Implement before/after comparison slider with acne-to-clear skin results and disclaimer
- Create testimonials section with 3 customer review cards featuring 5-star ratings
- Build blog preview section with 3 skincare tips article cards
- Add footer with newsletter signup, quick links, social icons, contact details, and payment icons
- Implement cart functionality allowing users to add products, update quantities, and proceed to checkout
- Create backend product catalog with 8+ sample products across 4 categories
- Apply consistent brand design system: soft beige (#F5EFE8), white, gold accents (#C9A96E), rounded corners, soft shadows, serif headings, sans-serif body text
- Ensure fully responsive design with mobile-first optimization, smooth scroll, and hover effects

**User-visible outcome:** Users can browse Cosmata's premium skincare products by category, view product details with clinical ingredient information, add items to cart, view before/after results, read customer testimonials and skincare tips, and proceed through a checkout flow. The site presents a clean, clinical, and luxurious aesthetic with complete mobile responsiveness.
